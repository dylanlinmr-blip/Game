How to play:
  • Drag the map to pan. Click your unit, then click adjacent hexes to move.
  • Hexes are black until explored, and dim when empty.
  • Click a capital, village, or city for recruit options; click an explored hex for build options.
  • Explore ruins for loot + technology.
  • Modes: Peaceful (no hostiles), Classic, King's Last Stand, and Campgain.
  • In King's Last Stand, your king must survive.
  • In Campgain, land at the ocean edge and conquer Ethus by taking its cities.
  • Pick Siege or Raid before starting a Campgain run.
  • Hostiles attack; allies occasionally appear for trade.
  • Win by exploring all hexes and defeating all hostiles.
